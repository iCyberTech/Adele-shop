Crafted in Light

Every DAASEBRE garment is conceived as a study in translucency. Our artisans work with bio-crystalline fabrics, refractive sequins and African-coated silk to create wearable light sculptures.

  • Bio-crystalline fabric technology
  • Hand-finished refractive detailing
  • Zero-waste Africancoating process
  • Carbon-neutral supply chain
