Woven in Gold

Every Daasebre piece begins in the village looms of the Volta region. Our master weavers use century-old techniques to create cloth that holds the wisdom of our elders.

  • Handwoven kente from Bonwire
  • Adinkra stamping with natural dyes
  • Ethical cooperatives, fair wages
  • Zero-waste loom techniques
